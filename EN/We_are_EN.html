We are!

The music "We are!" is the first oppening of One Piece and the most of the animation.

It was composed by Kohei Tanaka, sung by Hi-roshi Kitadani with lyrics written by Skoko Fujibayashi.

The music was composed especially for the manga. It is considered as the the main oppening of One Piece. It was used in all the arc of East Blue "first arc of one piece".

What does the music symbolize?

It is notably on several films of One Piece to remind the value of oppening: Joy.

Indeed, the oppening of One Piece symbolizes the motivation and the achievement of our dreams. This corresponds exactly to the values of the animation.

With a main character who unreal number of dreams, he shows us that everything is possible by doing everything doing everything to make them come true.
