Transmission of message through mangas

Manga is read for the positive messages it conveys. The stories are unique and are much more extensive than a comic book that is around 50 pages.

Happy Luffy

Example on One Piece

They are often comical with a protagonist who is in the style of idiocy just like Luffy. Besides, our future king of the pirates transmits positive emotions.

While keeping a story as incredible with incredible story, with just such a takeover of reality to convey a message. We saw this in the arc of Wano.

Manga often reflect reality. The stories remind us of the rules of friendship, give hope like Luffy who will never give up and will always do what is necessary to necessary to save his teammates even if he had to lose his life.

This gives a very nice message to the readers and reminds them of the importance of friendship.

This makes the beauty of these Japanese books that announces itself to all tastes. For the little ones, for the big ones, for the girls, for the boys...
